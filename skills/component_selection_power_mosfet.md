# Skill: Power MOSFET Selection

Use when ranking MOSFET, GaN, or SiC power devices.

Inputs:
- Voltage/current stress
- Switching frequency
- Thermal limits
- Package and footprint constraints

Steps:
- Check Vds/current/SOA margin.
- Compare Rds_on at temperature, Qg, Coss/Eoss, reverse recovery, package thermal data, availability, price, model availability, and footprint risk.
- Mark mock or missing datasheet values.

Human approval:
- Required before selecting final power semiconductors.
