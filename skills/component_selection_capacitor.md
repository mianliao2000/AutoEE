# Skill: Capacitor Selection

Use when selecting input/output capacitors for power converters.

Inputs:
- DC voltage, ripple current, ripple voltage target, ambient temperature, layout constraints.

Steps:
- Apply voltage derating and DC-bias derating.
- Check capacitance, ESR, ESL, RMS current, lifetime, package, price, and availability.
- Record whether data is datasheet-backed or estimated.

Checks:
- Capacitor ESR loss exists or is marked missing.
- RMS current margin exists or is marked missing.
